The Best Chocolate Cake Recipe

You, too can enjoy this deliciousness

Chocolate finds its way into an endless array of desserts, from croissants and clafoutis to tarts and truffles. But perhaps its highest calling in the pantheon of American desserts is cake. An old-fashioned layer cake, to be exact, where tender forkfuls of cake get squashed up against just a bit too much fluffy chocolate buttercream.

The kind of cake that makes you want a tall glass of cold milk, even though you haven't had one in years. Ready fora slice? Here's how the ultimate chocolate cake comes together.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 spoons of sugar
  • 2 bags of cocoa
  • 2 bags of chocolate-chip
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 bag of vanilla
  • Sugar powder

Directions

  1. Mix the milk, flour and sugar
  2. Add the cocoa, chocolate and vanilla, mix again
  3. heat the oven to 200 degrees
  4. put the mixed thing in the oven for 30 minutes
  5. Add the sugar powder above the cake
